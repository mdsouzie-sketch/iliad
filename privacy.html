What we collect

When you fill out the booking form on this site, we collect the information you provide — name, email, phone (if entered), sport or activity, your stated goal, preferred dates, and any notes you write. When you visit the assessment in person, we additionally collect performance data from our testing equipment (force plate readings, sprint splits, movement screen scores), training history, and contact information needed to schedule follow-up sessions.

How we use it

We use this information to schedule your appointment, deliver your assessment report, build your training program, and communicate with you about your engagement. Your performance data is used to calibrate your programming and track your progress over time.

Who we share it with

We don't sell or rent your information. We share it with: the practitioners on the ILIAD team working with you; our scheduling and email tools (e.g. email provider, calendar software, payment processor) only to the extent needed to operate the business; and as required by law. We do not share your performance data with third parties without your explicit written consent.
