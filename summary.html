ILIAD is a performance testing and training facility built on a single principle: test before you train. We measure what other gyms guess at — force production, sprint phases, movement quality — and we program from data instead of templates.

The Method
Most training is hope. Ours is method.

Every athlete starts with a 90-minute diagnostic. Every program is calibrated from the data the diagnostic produces. Every six weeks we re-test to confirm the work is producing the change we expected.

Testing — The Diagnostic
90 minutes. Three protocols.
  • I.
    Force Plate Testing
    CMJ, single-leg, isometric pull. Power asymmetry exposed at 1000Hz.
  • II.
    Speed Analysis
    0–10 / 10–20 / 20–30 yard splits + 5-10-5 pro-agility. Phase-by-phase.
  • III.
    Movement Screening
    7-pattern FMS with video, asymmetry score, joint-level mobility flags.
Programs — From Diagnosis to Discipline
Four programs, one philosophy.
  • I.
    Performance Coaching
    1:1 sport-specific programming for the competitive athlete.
  • II.
    Personal Training
    General strength training for function and longevity.
  • III.
    Corrective Exercise
    Targeted intervention for asymmetries the screen revealed.
  • IV.
    Recovery & Stretching
    Assisted stretching and structured recovery — the work between the work.
