After the assessment.

Programs are calibrated to your data. Two athletes with the same goal often get very different prescriptions — and very different prices. Once we've reviewed your assessment results, we'll discuss which program fits, what cadence makes sense, and what it costs. The conversation happens during your 30-minute results consult, included with the assessment.

Performance Coaching

Weekly 1:1 coached sessions, sport-specific programming, continuous data tracking, re-tests every 4–6 weeks. For competitive athletes whose performance has measurable consequences.

Personal Training

General strength training built on your diagnostic foundation, for adults who want to train seriously without competition prep. Per-session or session packs.

Corrective Exercise

Targeted intervention for the asymmetries the screen revealed; runs alongside another program until the patterns clear. Typically a focused 4–12 week block.

Recovery & Stretching

Assisted stretching, mobility flows, and structured soft-tissue work. Available as drop-ins or as a recovery package alongside any program.

Teams & Roster Assessments

Custom pricing for teams testing 10+ athletes. Includes a team-level summary report identifying roster-wide injury risk and performance gaps. Contact us for a quote.

FAQ

Common questions.

Do I have to enroll in a program?

No. The assessment is a one-time fee with no obligation. You can take the report and use it however you want — with us, with your existing coach, or on your own.

Why isn't program pricing on this page?

Because programs aren't one-size. Two athletes with the same goal often get very different prescriptions, and pricing follows what the program needs to do. Once we have your assessment data, we can recommend the right program and quote a fair number — that conversation happens during your results consult.

What does the assessment include vs. cost extra?

The price you see covers everything listed under "What's included" above — full protocols, written report, video review, and the results consult. There are no upsells during the assessment itself.

What if I'm not local?

The assessment requires you to be on-site for the testing — no remote option for that. Once you have a baseline report, follow-up programming and check-ins can be delivered remotely; re-tests still require an in-person visit.
